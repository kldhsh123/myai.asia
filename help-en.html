Introduction

Welcome to my AI help document. This document is designed to help you resolve various issues you may encounter during use.

Issue 1: The page displays entirely in English including `Just a moment...`

Solution:

  1. Refresh the page.
  2. Try visiting https://www.myai.asia and then redirect to the main site.

Possible Reason: The page triggered the Cloudflare firewall or requires revalidation (usually every 30 minutes).

Issue 2: The page displays a long string of information including "Cheerful Technology Firewall"

Solution:

  1. Close the current chat window and open a new chat.

Possible Reason: The chat content contained potentially harmful content that triggered the firewall (mostly fixed).

Issue 3: Displays insufficient remaining credits or similar messages

Solution:

Contact the group owner, join the discord https://discord.gg/CKrvgCyK4y, and report your issue.

Possible Reason: It could be due to a channel error or insufficient site quota.

Issue 4: Various flashy error messages appear

Solution:

  1. Try using translation tools to resolve it yourself.
  2. Join the discord https://discord.gg/CKrvgCyK4y and report your issue.

Possible Reason: Errors caused by unknown reasons may need further communication to resolve.

Issue 5: "No available channels for model *** in current group vip"

Solution:

  1. Join the discord https://discord.gg/CKrvgCyK4y and report your issue.

Possible Reason: The API corresponding to this model may be down; wait for recovery.

We hope the above information helps you smoothly use our services. For further assistance or other questions, please feel free to contact us.
